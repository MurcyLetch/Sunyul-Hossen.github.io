WHO AM I?

I have to admit, I am a tech geek. I wasn’t always this way. You and I are probably similar. Like most of you, I owned a brick phone or a flip phone at one point. I was guilty as charged, yet at the time I did not care. I was using it as a way to call for help if I was to ever be stranded on the roads in the middle of nowhere. The brick or flip was often buried at the bottom of my purse or left at home because I just wasn’t in the habit of always being connected. Fast forward, here we are approaching 2020. Wow, look how we have evolved! We are dependent on smart phones loaded up with steroids/apps, tablets, smart watches, GPS devices that are connected to innovative devices to help us see our efficiencies or inefficiencies. Technology is being used in amazing ways. Autonomous bots and self-driving cars are something which always fascinated me. I love being in an industry that allows me to see all aspects of the CS world. It allows me to see both the creative to the analytics I do competitive programming as it's a fun stuff for me. In leisure time i love to sing and dance and paint fantasies.
